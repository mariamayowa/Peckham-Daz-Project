Welcome to Taste of Lagos – a mobile Essex based Nigerian catering company, serving London, Essex and Kent. Taste of Lagos’ most popular food item is its authentic Nigerian Shawarma, which tastes like it is straight from Lagos.

More Information

Straight off the grill (aka Live Catering) Taste of Lagos is prepared right in front of you, so you can say how you want it and how you like it – call it full autonomy. Whatever you order will already be pre-packaged in Taste of Lagos’ original packaging and it will be delivered pre-packaged to your event. This service is for the smallest gatherings to the much larger celebrations. We offer Shawarma platters & large trays of our other food items.
